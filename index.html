Project B: Ray Tracing

Qifan Yang: QYL123

User Instructions

- Drag mouse to rotate the camera. - WebGL Scene Preview on the left; Ray-Traced Result on the right. - Use the control panel on the right side to adjust most of the settings.

Keyboard Instructions

- 'T': Generate Ray Tracing image on canvas.
- 'C': Clear Ray Tracing image on canvas.

- W/S keys: move camera forward/backwards; - A/D keys: strafe left/right; - Q/E: strafe down/up.
